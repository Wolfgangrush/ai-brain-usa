# Purchase Order Terms and Conditions

## Source URLs
- [UCC Article 2 (Sales)](https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2)
- [UCC § 2-207 (battle of the forms)](https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2/2-207)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Common-law contract + UCC Article 2.

## Currency Warning
For international sales, CISG applies unless excluded by Art. 6. If CISG governs, UCC § 2-207 displaced; CISG Art. 19 mirror-image rule controls counter-offer / acceptance analysis.

## Template Type
Standard Commercial Contract — Buyer-Issued.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Order Header** — PO number; date; vendor; buyer ship-to / bill-to.
2. **Description of Goods** — quantity; SKU; description; unit price; total.
3. **Delivery** — date; INCOTERMS or domestic equivalent; partial shipment treatment.
4. **Payment Terms** — net 30 / 45 / 60 / 90; early-payment discount; payment method.
5. **Title and Risk of Loss** — passes per INCOTERMS / FOB / DDP.
6. **Warranties** — express warranty of conformity to specification; non-infringement; merchantability and fitness for ordinary purpose preserved.
7. **Inspection and Acceptance** — right to inspect; revocation of acceptance per UCC § 2-608.
8. **Cure** — vendor's right to cure non-conforming delivery under UCC § 2-508.
9. **Indemnification** — IP infringement; product defect; compliance with law.
10. **Insurance** — coverage minima.
11. **Compliance** — anti-corruption (FCPA); anti-slavery; export control; conflict minerals; California Transparency in Supply Chains Act; OFAC.
12. **Confidentiality** — buyer-confidential information.
13. **Termination** — for convenience; for cause.
14. **No Variation by Vendor Acknowledgment** — anti-battle-of-the-forms clause: this PO is the offer; vendor's acceptance limited to these terms; additional/different terms in vendor's acknowledgment expressly rejected.
15. **Governing Law · Forum · Notices · Assignment.**

## Drafting Conventions
- For battle of the forms (UCC § 2-207), include explicit "limited to terms of this PO" + clause rejecting any additional/different terms in vendor's acknowledgment.
- For international, include CISG exclusion if UCC desired.
- Specify INCOTERMS year (e.g., INCOTERMS 2020) to avoid version confusion.
- For high-value goods, include letter-of-credit terms or specific payment-security mechanisms.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "A definite and seasonable expression of acceptance or a written confirmation which is sent within a reasonable time operates as an acceptance even though it states terms additional to or different from those offered or agreed upon, unless acceptance is expressly made conditional on assent to the additional or different terms." — UCC § 2-207(1).
