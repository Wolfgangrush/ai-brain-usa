## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Common law (services-predominant) + state-specific cloud-services and privacy law overlay.

## Currency Warning
SaaS Agreement is licence + services hybrid; California, Texas, Massachusetts, and Washington each have specific consumer-protection / breach-notification overlays that flow through to enterprise SaaS.

## Template Type
Subscription / Cloud Services Contract.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties + Effective Date + Recitals.**
2. **Definitions** — Subscription Services · Subscription Term · Authorized Users · Customer Data · Documentation · Confidential Information · Personal Information.
3. **Subscription Grant** — non-exclusive, non-transferable, term-limited right to access and use; user-tier limits.
4. **Restrictions** — no reverse engineering · no benchmarking publication · no competitive use · no resale · no scraping.
5. **Customer Responsibilities** — Authorized User compliance · accurate data · lawful use · credentials security.
6. **Implementation and Onboarding** — milestones · acceptance · training.
7. **Service Levels (SLA)** — uptime metric · planned-maintenance carve-out · service credits · sole remedy.
8. **Fees and Payment** — subscription fee · usage overages · auto-renew · price-escalator cap · taxes.
9. **Customer Data** — Customer retains ownership; vendor receives limited licence to host, process, secure; return / deletion on termination; export format.
10. **Privacy and Security** — DPA incorporated; SOC 2 Type II / ISO 27001 commitments; breach notice within agreed hours; sub-processor list and consent.
11. **Confidentiality** — definition · term · exceptions · injunctive relief.
12. **Intellectual Property** — vendor retains IP in Services; Customer retains IP in Customer Data; feedback licence.
13. **Warranties** — performs materially as documented; complies with law; no malicious code; no open-source-IP encumbrance affecting Customer.
14. **Indemnification** — IP infringement defence + indemnify (procure rights / modify / refund); customer indemnity for Customer Data unlawfulness.
15. **Limitation of Liability** — exclude consequential / lost profits; cap = fees in prior 12 months; carve-out: confidentiality, IP indemnity, gross negligence, willful misconduct, breach of privacy obligations (often uncapped or super-capped).
16. **Term and Termination** — initial · auto-renewal · termination for material breach with cure · termination for convenience (rarely vendor-favourable) · effect (data export window).
17. **Suspension** — late payment · security incident · violation of acceptable use.
18. **Force Majeure · Assignment · Notices · Governing Law · Forum · Counterparts · Electronic Signature.**

## Drafting Conventions
- Service Levels: distinguish *Service Credits* (financial) from *Sole Remedy* (caps recovery); enterprise customers push back on sole-remedy framing.
- Sub-processor consent: notice-and-objection (vendor-friendly) vs. prior-written-consent (customer-friendly).
- Data deletion: agree on retention period after termination (typically 30-90 days) for export, then deletion within agreed timeframe + certificate of destruction on request.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "Reservation of title in goods or retention or rejection of goods by the buyer shall be the buyer's responsibility…" — UCC § 2-509 / contract analog: data-residency assumption resides with the party in possession.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Highest-frequency SaaS-vendor and SaaS-customer engagement. Pair with DPA (§ 04 BAA for HIPAA path) + SOC 2 / ISO 27001 audit-rights schedule + SLA schedule.
