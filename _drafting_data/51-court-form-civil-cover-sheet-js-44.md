# Civil Cover Sheet (Form JS-44)

## Source URLs
- [Administrative Office of U.S. Courts — JS 44 Civil Cover Sheet](https://www.uscourts.gov/forms/civil-forms/civil-cover-sheet)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Administrative Office — required by Local Rules of each U.S. District Court.

## Currency Warning
JS-44 is for statistical purposes only and does not supplement or replace pleadings. Check district-specific local form (some districts use modified JS-44 variants).

## Template Type
Administrative Form (filed with complaint or removal).

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Section I — Plaintiff and Defendant** — name, county of residence (excluding county of U.S. Plaintiff cases), attorney information.
2. **Section II — Basis of Jurisdiction** — U.S. Government Plaintiff / U.S. Government Defendant / Federal Question / Diversity.
3. **Section III — Citizenship of Principal Parties** (if diversity) — table by Plaintiff / Defendant.
4. **Section IV — Nature of Suit** — code (contract / tort / real property / civil rights / IP / labor / immigration / prisoner / bankruptcy / other statutes); coded list.
5. **Section V — Origin** — original / removed / remanded / reinstated / transferred / MDL.
6. **Section VI — Cause of Action** — cite U.S. Civil Statute under which filing (don't cite jurisdictional statutes unless diversity) + brief description.
7. **Section VII — Requested in Complaint** — class action checkbox; demand $; jury trial checkbox.
8. **Section VIII — Related Cases** — if any.
9. **Signature and Date.**

## Drafting Conventions
- File with complaint or notice of removal.
- Use accurate nature-of-suit code — affects judicial assignment in some districts.
- For diversity cases, complete Section III citizenship of principal parties.
- Related-cases disclosure can affect judge assignment.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "The JS 44 civil cover sheet and the information contained herein neither replaces nor supplements the filing and service of pleadings or other papers as required by law, except as provided by local rules of court." — Form JS 44 header.

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Companion to every complaint and notice of removal.
