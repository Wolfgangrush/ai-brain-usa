# Certificate of Incorporation — Delaware

## Source URLs
- [DGCL § 102 (contents)](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title8/c001/sc02/index.html)
- [Delaware Division of Corporations](https://corp.delaware.gov)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Primary — DGCL Title 8.

## Currency Warning
DGCL § 102(b)(7) — director exculpation; 2022 amendment extended officer exculpation (limited carve-outs). § 122(18) (2024) — stockholder agreement governance provisions. Annual franchise tax + minimum corporate income tax for Delaware corporations.

## Template Type
Corporate Charter.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Name** — must be distinguishable on records of DE Division of Corporations; must include "Corporation," "Incorporated," "Limited," or abbreviation.
2. **Registered Agent and Office** — Delaware office address and agent.
3. **Purpose** — broad-purpose clause: "to engage in any lawful act or activity for which corporations may be organized under the General Corporation Law of Delaware."
4. **Authorized Capital Stock**:
   - Total number of shares authorized.
   - By class — Common Stock (number, par value); Preferred Stock (number, par value; "blank check" authority for board to designate series).
   - Rights, preferences, and limitations of each class.
5. **Incorporator** — name and address.
6. **Board of Directors** — initial directors (optional) or organizational meeting; classified board if applicable; stockholder removal restrictions.
7. **Stockholder Action by Written Consent** — permitted or expressly prohibited.
8. **Stockholder Meetings** — call rights.
9. **Indemnification** — broad indemnification of directors, officers, and others.
10. **Director Exculpation under § 102(b)(7)** — eliminate or limit director monetary liability for breach of fiduciary duty (except duty of loyalty / bad faith / § 174 violations / improper personal benefit).
11. **Officer Exculpation under § 102(b)(7) (2022)** — for senior officers, in claims other than by or in the right of the corporation.
12. **Amendment** — provisions governing amendment of certificate.
13. **Signature** by incorporator.

## Drafting Conventions
- "Blank check" preferred — board can designate series later by certificate of designation; common in venture-financed companies.
- Exclusive-forum-selection clause for internal-affairs claims (DGCL § 115) — Delaware Court of Chancery / DGCL-related state court.
- Federal-forum provision for Securities Act claims — *Salzberg v. Sciabacucchi*, 227 A.3d 102 (Del. 2020), upheld.
- "Section 220 carve-out" — recent trend: provisions limiting stockholder books-and-records demands; enforceability mixed.
- Avoid super-specific terms that constrain future financing rounds; build flexibility via preferred-stock designations.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "The certificate of incorporation shall set forth: (1) The name of the corporation, which… shall be distinguishable upon the records in the office of the Secretary of State…; (2) The address… of the corporation's registered office…; (3) The nature of the business or purposes to be conducted or promoted…; (4) … the total number of shares of stock that the corporation shall have authority to issue…; (5) The name and mailing address of the incorporator(s)…" — 8 Del. C. § 102(a).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Foundational corporate document. Pair with bylaws + initial board / written consent + Form D filing (for early-stage equity issuance) + Section 83(b) elections.
