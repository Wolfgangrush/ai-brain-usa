## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Common-law employment + FLSA / Title VII / state wage-and-hour overlay.

## Currency Warning
Non-compete enforceability shifted materially: FTC 2024 non-compete rule was permanently enjoined by *Ryan, LLC v. FTC*, No. 3:24-CV-986 (N.D. Tex. 20 Aug 2024); state law controls. Several states (CA, ND, OK, MN as of 2023, NY pending) effectively ban or severely limit non-competes; many others limit duration / geography / consideration.

## Template Type
Employment Contract — At-Will Offer Letter.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Date and Salutation.**
2. **Position and Reporting** — title · reporting line · location (or remote / hybrid) · expected start date.
3. **Compensation** — base salary (annual + pay-period) · classification under FLSA (exempt / non-exempt) · overtime entitlement disclosure if non-exempt.
4. **Bonus and Equity** — discretionary vs. plan-based bonus · target bonus % · equity grant subject to board approval + plan documents + vesting schedule (typically 4-year, 1-year cliff).
5. **Benefits** — health · dental · vision · 401(k) eligibility and match · PTO · sick leave · paid holidays · parental leave + state-specific paid family leave (CA / NY / NJ / others).
6. **Confidentiality + IP Assignment** — incorporates separate Proprietary Information and Inventions Agreement (PIIA); pre-existing inventions Schedule.
7. **Restrictive Covenants** — non-solicitation of employees + customers (state-law enforceability check); non-compete: include ONLY where enforceable under governing state law (see Currency Warning); garden leave alternative.
8. **At-Will Statement** — employment is at will; either party may terminate at any time with or without cause / notice.
9. **Background Check / I-9 / Drug Screen Contingencies.**
10. **Right to Work** — employment offer contingent on satisfactory completion of Form I-9 and employment eligibility verification.
11. **Acknowledgement of Policies** — employee handbook · equal employment opportunity · anti-harassment · acceptable-use.
12. **No Conflict** — representation that employee is not subject to any agreement that would conflict.
13. **Acceptance** — countersignature; offer expires by specified date.

## Drafting Conventions
- Avoid promising specific bonus / equity amounts as "guaranteed"; the at-will principle must be unambiguous.
- State-specific addenda: California requires written notice of wage rate and pay frequency (Wage Theft Prevention Act); New York requires similar notice (Labor Law § 195.1).
- For exempt classification, document the white-collar exemption test under 29 C.F.R. Part 541 — duties test + salary basis + salary level.
- Equity terms (vesting schedule, single/double trigger acceleration, cashless exercise) must be consistent with the Plan documents.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "Every employer shall pay to each of his employees who in any workweek is engaged in commerce or in the production of goods for commerce… wages at the following rate: not less than $7.25 an hour beginning July 24, 2009…" — FLSA § 206(a)(1).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Workhorse start-up employment offer letter. Pair with the PIIA, equity grant package (NSO/ISO/RSU), and a confirmation-of-receipt of the employee handbook.
