# Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Proceeding — Generic Petition

## Source URLs
- [5 U.S.C. §§ 554, 556, 557 (formal adjudication under APA)](https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/5/part-I/chapter-5)
- Agency-specific organic statutes and rules of practice.

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Primary — APA formal adjudication + agency-specific rules.

## Currency Warning
*SEC v. Jarkesy*, 603 U.S. 109 (2024), held SEC enforcement actions seeking civil penalties for securities fraud must be tried in federal court before a jury under the Seventh Amendment. Other agency ALJ proceedings similarly affected to varying degrees; verify each agency's current jurisdiction in light of *Jarkesy* / *Lucia v. SEC*, 585 U.S. 237 (2018) appointment doctrine.

## Template Type
Petition / Response in Agency Adjudication.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Caption** — agency tribunal name · case docket number · parties.
2. **Procedural Background** — agency action triggering proceeding; petitioner status.
3. **Jurisdictional Statement** — agency's adjudicatory authority; petitioner's standing.
4. **Statement of Facts.**
5. **Legal Arguments** — agency statutory and regulatory framework; precedent (agency adjudications, court decisions).
6. **Constitutional Challenges** (where applicable) — Article III concerns post-*Jarkesy*; Seventh Amendment jury-trial right; Appointments Clause; due process.
7. **Relief Requested.**
8. **Signature Block.**

## Common Agencies and Their ALJ Procedures
- **SEC** — Office of Administrative Law Judges (post-*Jarkesy* scope limited).
- **FERC** — Energy proceedings.
- **NLRB** — Labor proceedings (Title 29).
- **EPA** — Environmental enforcement.
- **Department of Labor / OSHA** — Whistleblower retaliation, wage-and-hour citation.
- **OSHA Review Commission** — Citation contests.
- **CFPB** — Financial consumer protection.
- **Federal Mine Safety and Health Review Commission**.
- **HHS / OMHA** — Medicare appeals.

## Drafting Conventions
- Agency-specific procedural rules govern — Federal Rules of Civil Procedure not directly applicable.
- Discovery limits vary by agency; pre-hearing exchange of witnesses and exhibits typical.
- ALJ initial decision + agency final-order appeal process + judicial review in court of appeals under APA § 706.
- For prevailing parties, Equal Access to Justice Act (EAJA, 5 U.S.C. § 504) fee shifting for entities meeting size and asset limits.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "This section applies… in every case of adjudication required by statute to be determined on the record after opportunity for an agency hearing…" — 5 U.S.C. § 554(a).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Variable practice depending on agency. Verify *Jarkesy* effect on the specific agency's adjudicatory authority before relying on ALJ as the sole forum.
