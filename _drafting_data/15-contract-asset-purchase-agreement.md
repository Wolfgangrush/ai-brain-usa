# Asset Purchase Agreement (APA)

## Source URLs
- [Delaware Code Title 6 — Article 9 Secured Transactions (UCC)](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title6/c009/index.html)
- [HSR Act — 15 U.S.C. § 18a](https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/15/18a)
- [Internal Revenue Code § 1060 (allocation of consideration)](https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26/1060)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Common-law contract + UCC Article 9 (bulk-sales repealed in most states) + HSR antitrust + IRC § 1060 allocation + applicable state successor-liability law.

## Currency Warning
Successor-liability doctrines vary state-by-state (de facto merger, mere continuation, fraudulent-transfer, product-line exception). California and some other jurisdictions apply product-line exception to assumption analysis. Environmental liabilities under CERCLA can pass to asset purchaser; cybersecurity due diligence increasingly material in valuation.

## Template Type
M&A Transaction Contract.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties + Recitals + Effective Date.**
2. **Definitions** — Acquired Assets · Assumed Liabilities · Excluded Assets · Excluded Liabilities · Closing · Material Adverse Effect · Knowledge.
3. **Purchase and Sale** — defined list of Acquired Assets; defined list of Excluded Assets; defined list of Assumed Liabilities; defined list of Excluded Liabilities.
4. **Purchase Price + Allocation** — cash, stock, earn-out, holdback / escrow; IRC § 1060 allocation schedule.
5. **Closing Mechanics** — deliverables · simultaneous or deferred · escrow · funds-flow.
6. **Representations and Warranties of Seller** — organization · authority · title to assets · liens · financial statements · undisclosed liabilities · litigation · contracts · IP · employees · benefits · tax · environmental · cyber/PII · compliance with law · privacy and data protection · insurance · brokers.
7. **Representations and Warranties of Buyer** — organization · authority · financing · independent investigation.
8. **Covenants between Signing and Closing** — operate in ordinary course · access to information · regulatory filings (HSR · CFIUS where applicable) · third-party consents · exclusivity.
9. **Conditions to Closing** — accuracy of representations · performance of covenants · no MAE · regulatory approvals · required consents.
10. **Indemnification** — survival periods · baskets/deductibles · caps · materiality scrapes · special indemnities (taxes · environmental · cyber · IP) · fundamental representations carve-out · escrow / R&W insurance.
11. **Tax Matters** — purchase-price allocation · transfer taxes · post-closing returns · cooperation.
12. **Employees** — offer of employment · transferred employees · benefits transition · WARN Act compliance (29 U.S.C. § 2101).
13. **Non-Compete + Non-Solicit** — applicable to Seller and key personnel; state-law enforceability.
14. **Termination Rights** — drop-dead date · breach · failure of condition; reverse breakup fees if any.
15. **Miscellaneous** — governing law · forum · notices · entire agreement · counterparts.

## Drafting Conventions
- Define Knowledge with named individuals (typically 3-5 of Seller's senior team).
- For data-handling targets, add specific cyber-incident representation and pre-closing forensic-audit covenant.
- HSR analysis at LOI stage; build the HSR filing schedule into Conditions if size-of-transaction threshold met (verify current threshold).
- For going-concern asset deals, Bulk Transfers under UCC Article 6 has been repealed in most states, but creditor notice may be required in any remaining-Article-6 jurisdiction.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "The transferee's basis in property acquired by purchase shall be its cost (such cost being apportioned among such items in the manner provided in section 1060 if the acquired assets constitute a trade or business)." — IRC § 1012 + § 1060 (combined effect).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Standard middle-market M&A deal. Pair with diligence questionnaire (or full data-room index), R&W insurance binder, and HSR filing checklist (where applicable).
