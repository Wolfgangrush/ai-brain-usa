# Board Resolutions / Written Consent

## Source URLs
- [DGCL § 141(f) (action by written consent)](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title8/c001/sc04/index.html)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Primary — DGCL § 141.

## Currency Warning
DGCL § 141(c) — committees of the board; DGCL § 141(f) — unanimous written consent by all directors typically required; some bylaws permit majority consent if so provided.

## Template Type
Corporate Governance Action Document.

## Structural Skeleton — Written Consent in Lieu of Meeting
1. **Title** — "UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF [COMPANY] IN LIEU OF MEETING."
2. **Recitals** — board's review of relevant materials; consultation with counsel and other advisors as applicable.
3. **Resolutions**:
   - **Approval of [Transaction / Action]** — "RESOLVED, that the Board hereby approves [action]."
   - **Authorization of Officers** — "RESOLVED, that [Officers] are authorized to execute and deliver all documents necessary or appropriate to consummate the foregoing."
   - **Specific Items** — adoption of plans, appointment of officers, issuance of shares, declaration of dividends, approval of stock options, approval of compensation, approval of M&A, approval of indebtedness, approval of subsidiary actions.
4. **General Authorization** — "RESOLVED, that any and all actions taken by any officer of the Company in connection with the foregoing matters be, and the same hereby are, ratified, confirmed and approved in all respects."
5. **Signature Block** — each director's signature; date.
6. **Filing in Corporate Minute Book.**

## Common Resolution Categories
- **Routine** — banking resolutions; officer election; appointment of auditor.
- **Financing** — approval of debt facility; approval of equity issuance; approval of warrant.
- **M&A** — approval of merger / acquisition / sale of assets.
- **Equity** — adoption of equity incentive plan; grant of options; approval of stock split / dividend / repurchase.
- **Compensation** — executive compensation; bonus plan adoption.
- **Litigation** — settlement approval; engagement of special counsel.
- **Related-Party Transactions** — disinterested-director approval under DGCL § 144.

## Drafting Conventions
- For unanimous written consent (DGCL § 141(f)), all directors must sign; can be in counterparts.
- Pre-approval recitals: document materials reviewed, advice obtained, board deliberation.
- For interested-director transactions, identify disinterested directors voting; recite material facts disclosed; meet DGCL § 144 framework or *MFW* path for business-judgment review.
- For Section 220 books-and-records demand response, board resolutions become discoverable; draft accordingly.
- Distinguish Board Resolutions (corporate governance) from Officer Certificates (factual statements signed by officer).

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "Unless otherwise restricted by the certificate of incorporation or bylaws, any action required or permitted to be taken at any meeting of the board of directors or of any committee thereof may be taken without a meeting if all members of the board or committee, as the case may be, consent thereto in writing, or by electronic transmission." — 8 Del. C. § 141(f).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Daily-use corporate-governance document. Foundation for documenting board action between meetings.
