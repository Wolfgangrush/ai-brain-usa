## Authority Tier
Primary — 28 U.S.C. §§ 1441, 1446, 1447 + jurisdictional bases (§ 1331, § 1332, CAFA § 1332(d)).

## Currency Warning
Notice of removal must be filed within 30 days after defendant's receipt of the initial pleading (28 U.S.C. § 1446(b)(1)); for cases that become removable later, 30 days from receipt of other paper from which removability first ascertainable (§ 1446(b)(3)). Diversity cases: 1-year outer limit from commencement (§ 1446(c)(1)) unless plaintiff acted in bad faith. *Murphy Bros., Inc. v. Michetti Pipe Stringing, Inc.*, 526 U.S. 344 (1999): formal service triggers clock, not informal receipt.

## Template Type
Procedural — Notice Filed in Federal District Court.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Caption** — Federal District Court · "[Plaintiff], Plaintiff, v. [Defendant], Defendant. NOTICE OF REMOVAL."
2. **Introduction** — "Defendant [name] hereby gives notice of removal of this action from the [state court] to this Court pursuant to 28 U.S.C. §§ 1441 and 1446."
3. **State Court Action** — caption · case number · filing date · service date.
4. **Timeliness** — date of service of initial pleading on removing defendant; 30-day clock satisfied.
5. **Jurisdictional Basis** — choose applicable:
   - **Federal Question (§ 1331)** — well-pleaded complaint shows federal-law claim.
   - **Diversity (§ 1332(a))** — complete diversity (allege citizenship of each party) + amount in controversy > $75,000.
   - **CAFA (§ 1332(d))** — minimal diversity + 100+ class members + $5M aggregate.
   - **Federal Officer (§ 1442)** — acting under federal officer / agency / corporation; colorable federal defense.
6. **All Defendants Consent (§ 1446(b)(2)(A))** — for non-CAFA / non-federal-officer cases, all properly served defendants must consent or join.
7. **Venue** — embracing the state court (28 U.S.C. § 1441(a)).
8. **Reservation of Defenses** — removal does not waive any defenses under Rules 12(b) other than venue.
9. **Notice to State Court (§ 1446(d))** — promptly file a copy in state court and serve all parties; state court loses jurisdiction.
10. **Required Attachments** — copies of all process, pleadings, and orders served on removing defendant in state court action.
11. **Signature Block.**

## Drafting Conventions
- Forum-defendant rule (§ 1441(b)(2)): in diversity cases, no removal if any properly served defendant is a citizen of the state where action brought.
- For corporations, citizenship is BOTH state of incorporation AND principal place of business (*Hertz Corp. v. Friend*, 559 U.S. 77 (2010)).
- For LLCs, citizenship is that of each member; plead each member's citizenship.
- Amount-in-controversy: by preponderance of evidence (*Dart Cherokee Basin Operating Co. v. Owens*, 574 U.S. 81 (2014)); reasonable inferences from complaint + petitioner's evidence.
- File companion: Civil Cover Sheet (JS-44), Corporate Disclosure (Rule 7.1), Local Rule "designation" of related cases if any.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "The notice of removal of a civil action or proceeding shall be filed within 30 days after the receipt by the defendant, through service or otherwise, of a copy of the initial pleading setting forth the claim for relief upon which such action or proceeding is based, or within 30 days after the service of summons upon the defendant if such initial pleading has then been filed in court and is not required to be served on the defendant, whichever period is shorter." — 28 U.S.C. § 1446(b)(1).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Time-sensitive procedural filing. Calendar 30-day clock at first contact; build removal analysis into intake. Standard companion: Motion to Stay or to Dismiss (Rule 12) timed to follow.
