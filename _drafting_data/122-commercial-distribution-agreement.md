## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Common-law contract + UCC + federal antitrust.

## Currency Warning
*Leegin Creative Leather Prods., Inc. v. PSKS, Inc.*, 551 U.S. 877 (2007), overruled per-se ban on resale price maintenance; vertical price restraints analyzed under Rule of Reason. Various state laws (Maryland, California) restrict RPM by statute.

## Template Type
Distribution / Reseller Contract.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties + Recitals + Effective Date + Term.**
2. **Definitions** — Products; Territory; Channel; Authorized Customer.
3. **Appointment** — exclusive / non-exclusive; geographic territory; product line.
4. **Distributor Obligations**:
   - Use best / commercially reasonable efforts to promote and sell.
   - Marketing plan / sales targets.
   - Training of personnel.
   - Brand standards and trade-dress compliance.
   - Service / warranty support.
5. **Supplier Obligations**:
   - Supply on terms; lead times; minimum order quantities.
   - Marketing support.
   - Training.
6. **Pricing**:
   - Wholesale pricing schedule.
   - Suggested resale price (NOT mandated under federal law; analyzed under Rule of Reason).
   - Most-favored-customer clause if any.
7. **Resale Restrictions** — territorial limits (lawful under Rule of Reason for non-naked restraints); customer-class limits.
8. **Trademark License** — limited license for marketing; quality control; termination revoking.
9. **Termination** — for-cause; for-convenience; effect on inventory (right of return / liquidated-damages); post-termination non-compete.
10. **Indemnification + Insurance + LOL.**
11. **Compliance** — FCPA + OFAC + anti-bribery + modern slavery + conflict minerals + export control.
12. **Confidentiality.**
13. **Antitrust Compliance** — counsel review of resale-price, exclusivity, and customer-allocation provisions.
14. **Dispute Resolution + Governing Law + Forum.**

## Drafting Conventions
- For exclusive territorial distribution, post-*Leegin* Rule of Reason analysis; document pro-competitive justifications.
- Robinson-Patman price-discrimination analysis if supplier sells to competing distributors at different prices.
- *Continental TV, Inc. v. GTE Sylvania, Inc.*, 433 U.S. 36 (1977), governs vertical non-price restraints under Rule of Reason.
- For minimum-resale-price terms, consider state-law restrictions and litigation risk; some clients elect not to impose minimum resale prices.
- For international distribution, address local competition law (EU vertical block exemption analog) + foreign-agent laws.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "Vertical price restraints are to be judged according to the rule of reason." — *Leegin Creative Leather Prods., Inc. v. PSKS, Inc.*, 551 U.S. 877, 907 (2007).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Standard manufacturer-distributor contract. Antitrust review of restraints is mandatory at draft stage.
