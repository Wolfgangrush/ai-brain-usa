## Authority Tier
DGCL + analog state corporation statutes; for LLCs, swap to Operating Agreement under LLC act.

## Currency Warning
DGCL 2024 amendments (in response to *West Palm Beach Firefighters' Pension Fund v. Moelis & Co.*) added § 122(18) explicitly authorizing stockholder agreements containing governance provisions formerly available only through certificate amendments. Affects allocation of board / management authority between certificate, bylaws, and stockholder agreement.

## Template Type
Corporate Governance Contract.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties + Recitals + Effective Date** — Company and each shareholder; coverage of future issuances by deemed-joinder clause.
2. **Definitions** — Investors · Founders · Common Holders · Preferred Holders · Affiliate · Permitted Transferee · Drag-Along Threshold.
3. **Board Composition and Voting** — designated seats (e.g., 2 by Investors · 2 by Founders · 1 independent jointly approved); quorum and consent requirements; observer seats.
4. **Protective Provisions** — supermajority or class-vote required for: amendments to charter · liquidation · M&A · debt > threshold · option-pool expansion · changes in number of directors · related-party transactions.
5. **Information Rights** — annual audited financials · quarterly unaudited · monthly management reports · budget approval · inspection rights.
6. **Pre-Emptive Rights / Right of First Offer (ROFO)** — on new issuances of equity or equity-linked securities to maintain pro rata ownership.
7. **Right of First Refusal (ROFR) on Transfers** — on Founder transfers (and sometimes Investor secondary transfers) to a non-permitted transferee.
8. **Co-Sale (Tag-Along) Rights** — Investors entitled to participate in Founder transfers above threshold.
9. **Drag-Along Rights** — on Approved Sale (defined: investor consent + holder threshold) all stockholders join.
10. **Lock-Up + Market Stand-Off** — IPO underwriter customary lock-up (180 days).
11. **Restrictions on Transfer** — securities-law compliance · transferee joinder · permitted transferees · prohibited transferees.
12. **Director and Officer Indemnification + D&O Insurance Coverage Floor.**
13. **Confidentiality** — including IP-related shared learnings.
14. **Termination Events** — IPO · acquisition · written consent.
15. **Miscellaneous** — governing law (Delaware) · forum (Delaware Court of Chancery) · entire agreement · amendments by class consent.

## Drafting Conventions
- Distinguish provisions that belong in the Certificate of Incorporation (must be in certificate) from those in the Bylaws or Shareholders Agreement. Conversion ratio + dividend preference + liquidation preference live in the Certificate.
- Drag-along clauses must include compliance with the Delaware "necessary part" doctrine for inclusion of restricted-Investor-class consent.
- Post-Moelis / § 122(18), governance provisions in stockholder agreements have express statutory anchor — confirm consent in Board resolution authorizing the agreement.
- For investor-favourable terms, layer Right of First Refusal + Co-Sale + Drag-Along + Pre-Emptive Rights so corporate-control balance is consistent across exits.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "Subject to the certificate of incorporation, a corporation, by action of the board of directors, may enter into contracts with one or more current or prospective stockholders… in such contract's own name and on its own behalf… and to comply with the terms of such contract." — 8 Del. C. § 122(18) (2024).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Standard venture-financing deliverable. Pair with Certificate of Incorporation amendment, Investors' Rights Agreement, Voting Agreement (or fold into single SHA), and Right of First Refusal & Co-Sale Agreement (or fold).
