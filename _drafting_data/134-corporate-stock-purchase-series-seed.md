## Currency Warning
For Reg D Rule 506(c), all purchasers must be accredited investors AND issuer must take reasonable steps to verify accredited status. Form D filed within 15 days of first sale; state notice filings vary in timing.

## Template Type
Venture Financing Equity Investment.

## Structural Skeleton — NVCA-Style Series Documents
1. **Stock Purchase Agreement (SPA)** — share count; purchase price; closing mechanics; representations.
2. **Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation** — Preferred Stock series with:
   - Liquidation preference (typically 1x non-participating).
   - Dividend preference.
   - Conversion ratio (initially 1:1; anti-dilution adjustment).
   - Anti-dilution protection (typically broad-based weighted average).
   - Protective provisions (supermajority / class-vote required for specified actions).
   - Voting rights (typically as-converted-to-common basis).
3. **Investors' Rights Agreement (IRA)** — registration rights (demand / piggyback / Form S-3); information rights; pre-emptive rights; affirmative covenants.
4. **Right of First Refusal and Co-Sale Agreement (ROFR/CSA)** — on Founder transfers.
5. **Voting Agreement** — board composition; drag-along provisions.
6. **Common Stock Purchase Agreement for Founders / Stock Restriction Agreement** — vesting (typically 4-year, 1-year cliff); right of repurchase.

## Drafting Conventions
- For Series Seed (smaller), simplified docs (Series Seed Documents); for Series A onward, full NVCA suite.
- Liquidation preference structure: non-participating (1x) vs. participating with cap vs. participating uncapped — increasingly market-standard non-participating.
- Anti-dilution: broad-based weighted average (favorable to founders); narrow-based / full-ratchet (favorable to investors).
- Protective provisions: list-of-actions requiring preferred-stock-class vote — typical: amend charter, declare dividends, M&A, liquidation, increase authorized shares.
- Drag-along threshold: typically majority of preferred + majority of common; common-stockholder appraisal rights waiver subject to enforceability.
- For Reg D Rule 506(c) public-solicited offerings, all-accredited + verification.
- For Reg D Rule 506(b) traditional placements, no public solicitation; up to 35 sophisticated non-accredited investors permitted.
- File Form D within 15 days of first sale.
