# Loan / Credit Agreement (Commercial)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Common-law contract + UCC Article 9 + LSTA market practice + bank-regulatory overlay.

## Currency Warning
LIBOR cessation: U.S. dollar LIBOR ceased for most tenors 30 June 2023; market migrated to SOFR (Secured Overnight Financing Rate). Verify rate-conversion provisions reference Alternative Reference Rates Committee (ARRC)-recommended fallbacks.

## Template Type
Loan Agreement / Term Sheet + Credit Facility.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties** — Borrower(s); Guarantor(s); Lender(s); Administrative Agent (syndicated).
2. **Definitions** — terms of art (LSTA standard).
3. **Facilities** — term loan(s); revolving credit; letter-of-credit subfacility; swingline.
4. **Interest Rate** — base rate + applicable margin; SOFR + spread adjustment + applicable margin (replace LIBOR references); benchmark replacement provisions.
5. **Mandatory Prepayments** — excess cash flow; asset sales; insurance proceeds; debt issuances; equity issuances.
6. **Voluntary Prepayments** — call premium / breakage costs / make-whole.
7. **Representations and Warranties** — organization; authorization; financial statements; no MAC; litigation; tax; environmental; ERISA; compliance with law; sanctions/anti-money-laundering/FCPA; intellectual property; insurance.
8. **Affirmative Covenants** — financial statements + compliance certificates; insurance; tax payment; permits; books and records; inspection; further assurances.
9. **Negative Covenants** — liens (negative pledge); debt; investments; restricted payments (dividends + repurchases); fundamental changes; asset sales; transactions with affiliates; restrictive agreements.
10. **Financial Covenants** — leverage ratio; interest coverage ratio; minimum liquidity; capex limits.
11. **Events of Default** — non-payment; covenant breach; cross-default; bankruptcy; ERISA; judgment; material adverse change.
12. **Remedies** — acceleration; foreclosure under UCC Article 9 (commercial reasonableness; notice; deficiency); receiver appointment.
13. **Collateral** — Security Agreement; Pledge Agreement; Mortgage / Deed of Trust; UCC-1 filings.
14. **Guaranties** — joint and several; subordination of intercompany debt.
15. **Intercreditor / Subordination Agreements** for senior / junior structures.
16. **Yield Protection** — increased costs; capital requirements; taxes; gross-up.
17. **Conditions Precedent to Initial Funding / Each Funding.**
18. **Miscellaneous** — assignments / participations; expenses; indemnification; governing law (typically NY); forum.

## Drafting Conventions
- Use ARRC-recommended fallbacks for benchmark rate cessation (Hardwired + Hedged Loan approach).
- Negative-pledge carve-outs (permitted liens) require precise drafting — Lien Basket vs. Specified Lien.
- Financial covenants tested quarterly; cure rights (equity cure) negotiated.
- For ABL facilities, borrowing-base certificate cadence + field-exam rights.
- ERISA representations — *Hughes v. Northwestern Univ.* doctrine + pension-fund-prudent-person standard.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "Subject to the satisfaction of the conditions precedent set forth in Section 4.01, each Lender severally agrees, on the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement, to make Loans to the Borrower from time to time…" — *typical LSTA-style loan provision*.
