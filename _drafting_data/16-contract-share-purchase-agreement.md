# Stock / Share Purchase Agreement (SPA)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Common-law contract + DGCL (typical) + federal securities + state blue-sky.

## Currency Warning
Reg D Rule 506(b) and 506(c) — 506(c) requires verified accredited-investor status; 506(b) permits self-certification but bars general solicitation. Form D filing within 15 days of first sale. Many states require notice filings (Blue Sky) within their own timeframes.

## Template Type
M&A Transaction Contract — Stock Purchase.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Parties + Recitals + Effective Date.**
2. **Definitions** — Shares · Closing · Material Adverse Effect · Knowledge · Indebtedness · Working Capital · Permitted Liens.
3. **Purchase and Sale of Shares** — purchase price (cash + stock + contingent · post-closing adjustment for net working capital · cash-free / debt-free).
4. **Closing Mechanics** — wire instructions · paying agent · escrow · funds-flow.
5. **Representations and Warranties of Seller(s)** — title to shares · authority · capitalization (cap table verbatim) · subsidiaries · financial statements · undisclosed liabilities · absence of changes · litigation · taxes · benefits + ERISA · employment · material contracts · IP · privacy/cyber · real property · environmental · compliance with law (FCPA · OFAC · export control · anti-trust) · permits/licences · related-party transactions · insurance · brokers.
6. **Representations and Warranties of Company** (where signing party).
7. **Representations and Warranties of Buyer** — authority · financing · investment intent + accredited-investor status (where Buyer receives stock as part of consideration).
8. **Covenants between Signing and Closing** — operate in ordinary course · negative covenants (no dividends · no IP assignment · no material contract · no settlements) · regulatory filings · third-party consents · exclusivity.
9. **Conditions to Closing** — accuracy + bring-down · performance · MAE · regulatory approvals (HSR / CFIUS) · consents · closing deliverables (resignations · releases · officer certificate · good-standing certificates).
10. **Indemnification** — survival periods · baskets · caps · fundamental reps carve-out · sandbagging clause · sole remedy except fraud and fundamental breaches · R&W insurance treatment.
11. **Tax Matters** — 338(h)(10) / 336(e) election analysis where applicable · transfer taxes · tax-sharing · transfer agent · cooperation · post-closing returns.
12. **Employee + Benefits Matters** — service crediting · benefit-plan transition · continuation · COBRA.
13. **Post-Closing Restrictive Covenants** — non-compete and non-solicit by Sellers (subject to state-law enforceability).
14. **Securities Compliance** — Seller's investment representations · resale restrictions · legending · Rule 144 awareness.
15. **Termination + Reverse Breakup Fee.**
16. **Miscellaneous.**

## Drafting Conventions
- Net Working Capital target definition is the most-disputed pre-closing term. Pre-closing analysis of three years of monthly NWC.
- For private-company shares paid in part with Buyer stock, ensure registration exemption (typically Reg D 506(c)) + state-blue-sky notice filings.
- For controlling-stockholder transactions, apply *Kahn v. M&F Worldwide* (MFW) six-step framework if seeking business-judgment review.
- Sandbagging clause — Delaware default is pro-sandbagging; explicit pro-sandbag or anti-sandbag clause controls.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "The board of directors of each corporation which desires to merge or consolidate shall adopt a resolution approving an agreement of merger or consolidation and declaring its advisability." — 8 Del. C. § 251(b) (M&A statutory analog for merger structure alternative to SPA).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Standard private-company acquisition document. Add R&W insurance schedule + Reg D Form D filing schedule + Blue Sky filing list as standing checklists.
