# SEC Form 8-K — Current Report

## Source URLs
- [SEC Form 8-K Instructions](https://www.sec.gov/files/form8-k.pdf)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Primary — Exchange Act § 13(a) + Form 8-K + Reg FD (17 C.F.R. § 243.100).

## Currency Warning
Item 1.05 (Material Cybersecurity Incidents) effective 18 December 2023 (smaller reporting companies 15 June 2024). Filing deadline: 4 business days from triggering event (or for Item 1.05, materiality determination).

## Template Type
Triggered Disclosure Filing.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Cover Page** — date of report; registrant; state of incorporation; commission file number; address; phone; check box for 12b-2 communications; trading symbol; emerging growth company indication.
2. **Item Number and Title** — disclose under specific Item (e.g., 1.01, 1.05, 2.01, 5.02).
3. **Body** — concise factual description of triggering event; material aspects.
4. **Exhibits** — material contracts; press releases (often furnished under Item 7.01 / 9.01); presentation materials.
5. **Forward-Looking Statements Disclaimer** under PSLRA.
6. **Signature.**

## Common Item Triggers
- **1.01** — Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
- **1.02** — Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.
- **1.03** — Bankruptcy or Receivership.
- **1.05** — Material Cybersecurity Incidents.
- **2.01** — Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.
- **2.02** — Results of Operations and Financial Condition (earnings releases).
- **2.03** — Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or Off-Balance-Sheet Arrangement.
- **2.04** — Triggering Events That Accelerate or Increase a Direct Financial Obligation.
- **2.05** — Costs Associated with Exit or Disposal Activities.
- **2.06** — Material Impairments.
- **3.01** — Notice of Delisting or Failure to Satisfy Continued Listing Rule.
- **3.02** — Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities.
- **4.01** — Changes in Registrant's Certifying Accountant.
- **4.02** — Non-Reliance on Previously Issued Financial Statements.
- **5.02** — Departure / Election of Directors; Compensation Decisions.
- **5.03** — Amendments to Articles or Bylaws.
- **5.07** — Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.
- **7.01** — Reg FD Disclosure (furnished, not filed).
- **8.01** — Other Events.
- **9.01** — Financial Statements and Exhibits.

## Drafting Conventions
- 4 business-day filing window from triggering event (Item 1.05: from materiality determination).
- Item distinguishes "filed" (subject to Section 18 liability) vs. "furnished" (not subject to Section 18). Furnished items typically 2.02 and 7.01.
- Reg FD compliance: avoid selective disclosure of material non-public information.
- For cybersecurity incidents, balance disclosure with non-disclosure of investigation specifics; Item 1.05 expressly does not require investigation-impairing details.
- Avoid Item 8.01 catch-all when specific Item applies.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "A report on this Form is required to be filed or furnished, as the case may be, upon the occurrence of any one or more of the events specified in the items in Sections 1 — Section 6 and Section 9 of this Form. Unless otherwise specified, a report is to be filed or furnished within four business days after occurrence of the event." — Form 8-K, General Instruction B.1.
