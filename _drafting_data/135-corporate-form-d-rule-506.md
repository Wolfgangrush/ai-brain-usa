# Form D — Notice of Exempt Offering (Reg D)

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Primary — Securities Act § 4(a)(2) safe harbor under Rule 506 + state notice-filing requirements.

## Currency Warning
Rule 506(c) accredited-investor verification + 506(b) general-solicitation prohibition. Form D no later than 15 calendar days after first sale (Rule 503).

## Template Type
SEC Notice Filing + State Blue-Sky Notice Filings.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Item 1 — Issuer's Identity** — name; jurisdiction; phone; year of incorporation.
2. **Item 2 — Principal Place of Business.**
3. **Item 3 — Related Persons** — executive officers; directors; promoters.
4. **Item 4 — Industry Group.**
5. **Item 5 — Issuer Size** — revenue range; aggregate net asset value.
6. **Item 6 — Federal Exemptions** — Rule 504 / Rule 506(b) / Rule 506(c) / Securities Act § 4(a)(5).
7. **Item 7 — Type of Filing** — new notice / amendment.
8. **Item 8 — Duration of Offering.**
9. **Item 9 — Type(s) of Securities** — equity / debt / option / warrant / pooled investment.
10. **Item 10 — Business Combination Transaction** (if applicable).
11. **Item 11 — Minimum Investment Accepted** from any outside investor.
12. **Item 12 — Sales Compensation** — recipient; CRD number; states involved.
13. **Item 13 — Offering and Sales Amounts** — total offering; total sold to date; date of first sale.
14. **Item 14 — Investors** — number accredited / non-accredited.
15. **Item 15 — Sales Commissions and Finder's Fees.**
16. **Item 16 — Use of Proceeds.**
17. **Signature** by authorized officer.

## State Notice Filings
- Concurrently with or shortly after Form D filing, file notice in each state where investors reside.
- State notice fees vary ($50–$1,500+).
- Some states have specific forms (NY Form 99 for some offerings; CA Form 25102(f) for Rule 506).
- NASAA's CRD/IARD systems used by many states.

## Drafting Conventions
- File Form D no later than 15 calendar days after first sale (Rule 503(a)).
- Amendments: required annually if offering continues, on material change, or to correct material error.
- For Rule 506(c), document accredited-investor verification (third-party verification letter, K-1 review, bank statements + brokerage statements + tax returns).
- "Bad actor" disqualification (Rule 506(d)) — verify all covered persons (officers, directors, 20% holders, promoters, broker-dealers, etc.) are not disqualified.
- For multi-investor closings, track total raised against offering ceiling.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "An issuer offering or selling securities in reliance on § 230.504 or 230.506 must file with the Commission a notice of sales containing the information required by Form D… for each new offering of securities no later than 15 calendar days after the first sale of securities in the offering." — 17 C.F.R. § 230.503(a).
