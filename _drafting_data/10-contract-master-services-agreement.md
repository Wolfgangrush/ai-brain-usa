# Master Services Agreement (MSA)

## Source URLs
- [Restatement (Second) of Contracts](https://www.ali.org/publications/show/contracts/) — common-law backstop.
- [UCC Article 2 (where goods predominate)](https://www.law.cornell.edu/ucc/2) — UCC § 2-207, § 2-314 cross-references.

## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Common-law contract + UCC Article 2 for mixed sales-of-goods components.

## Currency Warning
Choice-of-law clause typically Delaware, New York, or California. Each varies on liquidated-damages enforceability, limitation-of-liability ceilings, indemnification scope, and consequential-damages waivers.

## Template Type
Commercial Contract — Master Form.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Recitals + Effective Date + Parties.**
2. **Definitions** — Services · Statement of Work · Confidential Information · Deliverables · Personal Information · Affiliates.
3. **Scope of Services** — by SOW; MSA is the umbrella, SOWs the deliverables-and-fees layer.
4. **Fees and Payment** — fixed-fee · T&E · milestone · net-payment terms · late-fee · expense-reimbursement.
5. **Term and Termination** — initial term · renewal · termination for convenience · termination for cause + cure period · effect of termination.
6. **Confidentiality** — definition · obligations · exceptions · term beyond expiration · destruction/return.
7. **Intellectual Property** — pre-existing IP retention · ownership of deliverables (work-for-hire + assignment) · licence-back · feedback licence.
8. **Data Privacy and Security** — Personal Information handling · Data Processing Addendum incorporated · breach notice timing.
9. **Representations and Warranties** — authority · non-infringement · service standard · compliance with law · open-source disclosure.
10. **Indemnification** — IP infringement (defence + indemnify + procurement-of-rights or replacement) · breach of confidentiality · violation of law.
11. **Limitation of Liability** — exclusion of consequential / indirect / lost profits · liability ceiling (typically fees paid in prior 12 months); carve-outs for confidentiality, IP indemnity, gross negligence, willful misconduct.
12. **Insurance** — commercial general · professional/E&O · cyber-liability · workers comp · auto · umbrella; AI insured + waiver of subrogation.
13. **Independent Contractor** + no joint venture + no third-party beneficiaries.
14. **Assignment** — written consent; permitted M&A assignment.
15. **Force Majeure.**
16. **Governing Law and Forum** — exclusive jurisdiction.
17. **Dispute Resolution** — escalation · mediation · arbitration (AAA / JAMS) or litigation.
18. **General** — notices · entire agreement · amendments in writing · severability · counterparts · electronic signature · order of precedence (SOW > MSA when SOW expressly amends).

## Drafting Conventions
- Order-of-precedence clause must explicitly state SOW-controls-on-conflict-only-when-SOW-says-so to prevent inadvertent overrides.
- Limitation of liability cap should reference SOW-specific fees, not entire MSA.
- IP carve-out from LOL cap is common but increasingly negotiated to capped indemnity in vendor-friendly markets.
- For SaaS, swap "Deliverables" for "Subscription Services" and add uptime SLA + service credits.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "A contract may be made in any manner sufficient to show agreement, including conduct by both parties which recognizes the existence of such a contract." — UCC § 2-204(1) (where Article 2 applies).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Highest-volume commercial contract for a transactional practice. Pair with a SOW template and a Data Processing Addendum so the package is delivery-ready.
