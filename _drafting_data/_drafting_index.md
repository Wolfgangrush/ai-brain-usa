## Federal-First Anchor
All templates anchored in federal statutes, federal rules, and federal-procedure doctrine. State-procedural overlays are deferred to v0.3+ per v0.1 SCOPE.md. Delaware corporate law is included because most U.S. public companies and a large share of private companies are Delaware-incorporated, making DGCL effectively a federal corporate-law backbone.
