## Last Verified
2026-05-29

## Authority Tier
Primary — Exchange Act § 13(a) + Reg S-K + Reg S-X + Form 10-K Instructions.

## Currency Warning
Reg S-K Item 106 (cybersecurity risk management, strategy, governance) effective 18 December 2023. Climate-related disclosures pending litigation (*Chamber of Commerce v. SEC* / Eighth Circuit consolidated petitions). Pay-for-performance disclosure under Item 402(v).

## Template Type
Periodic Filing Shell — Public Company.

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Cover Page** — fiscal year end; commission file number; registrant; state of incorporation; IRS Employer ID; principal executive offices; phone; securities registered; large/accelerated/non-accelerated filer designation.
2. **PART I**:
   - **Item 1 — Business** — operations; products/services; competition; intellectual property; regulation; human capital management; cybersecurity strategy & governance (Item 106 cross-reference).
   - **Item 1A — Risk Factors** (Item 105).
   - **Item 1B — Unresolved Staff Comments.**
   - **Item 1C — Cybersecurity** (Item 106) — risk management & strategy; governance.
   - **Item 2 — Properties.**
   - **Item 3 — Legal Proceedings.**
   - **Item 4 — Mine Safety Disclosures** (if applicable).
3. **PART II**:
   - **Item 5 — Market for Registrant's Common Equity.**
   - **Item 6 — [Reserved]** (eliminated for emerging growth companies).
   - **Item 7 — MD&A** (Item 303) — results of operations; liquidity; capital resources; critical accounting estimates; non-GAAP measures.
   - **Item 7A — Quantitative and Qualitative Disclosures About Market Risk.**
   - **Item 8 — Financial Statements and Supplementary Data** (Reg S-X).
   - **Item 9 — Changes in and Disagreements with Accountants.**
   - **Item 9A — Controls and Procedures** — DC&P + ICFR; SOX § 404 attestation.
   - **Item 9B — Other Information.**
   - **Item 9C — Disclosure Regarding Foreign Jurisdictions That Prevent Inspections.**
4. **PART III** (incorporated by reference from proxy statement):
   - **Item 10 — Directors, Executive Officers, and Corporate Governance.**
   - **Item 11 — Executive Compensation** (Item 402).
   - **Item 12 — Security Ownership.**
   - **Item 13 — Certain Relationships and Related Transactions.**
   - **Item 14 — Principal Accountant Fees and Services.**
5. **PART IV**:
   - **Item 15 — Exhibits, Financial Statement Schedules.**
   - **Item 16 — Form 10-K Summary** (optional).
6. **Signatures** — registrant + principal executive officer + principal financial officer + at least majority of directors.
7. **Exhibits** — certifications under SOX §§ 302 & 906 (Exhibits 31.1, 31.2, 32.1, 32.2); material contracts; subsidiary list; consent of accountants.

## Drafting Conventions
- Risk factors: specific, not boilerplate; ordered by importance; refresh annually.
- MD&A: management's perspective; trend analysis; known material trends, demands, commitments, events, uncertainties.
- Non-GAAP measures: reconciliation to nearest GAAP measure required (Reg G).
- Cybersecurity disclosure: describe processes for assessment, identification, management of material risks; board oversight; management role.
- Pay-vs-performance table (Item 402(v)) for proxy-incorporated executive comp section.

## Fair-Use Excerpt
> "The annual report shall include … (i) a list of all subsidiaries of the registrant, the state or other jurisdiction of incorporation or organization of each, and the names under which such subsidiaries do business…" — Form 10-K, Part IV, Item 15.

Annual public-company disclosure deliverable. Coordinate with auditor, financial-reporting team, and external counsel for legal-proceedings and risk-factor sections.
