## Notes on Authority

- Federal statutes are cited from the Office of Law Revision Counsel (Title and section). Always verify current text at [uscode.house.gov](https://uscode.house.gov) before relying.
- State-law digests in this corpus (DGCL · UCC as a model code) MUST be verified against the enacting State's actual code text before use in any pleading.
- Doctrinal currents are referenced through Supreme Court decisions. Always check post-decision developments — circuit splits, lower-court applications, and pending review.
- ABA Formal Opinions are persuasive, not binding. The binding rule is each State's enacted Rules of Professional Conduct (or California Rules of Professional Conduct).
- Treaty obligations (CISG) are self-executing federal law where the Senate has ratified — but Article 95 declarations by the United States affect Article 1(1)(b) applicability.

## Currency

This corpus reflects authoritative law as of 2026 ship date. Update cycles:
- FRCP / FRE / FRCrP / FRAP — typically annual amendments effective 1 December.
- Constitutional doctrine — track Supreme Court October Term cycle.
- APA — track post-*Loper Bright* deference framework evolution.
- Securities regulation — track SEC final rule publications.
- HSR thresholds — revised annually by FTC.
- FLSA salary thresholds — track DOL final rules and litigation.
