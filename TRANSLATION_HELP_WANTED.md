# Translation Help Wanted

The AI Brain USA ships with 7 language guides. These translations are AI-assisted and need human review from native speakers who are also legal professionals. If you can help, please open a PR or issue.

## Languages and status

| Language | Status | Reviewer needed |
|---|---|---|
| English (authoritative) | Complete | Native review welcome |
| Spanish (Español) | AI-assisted draft | Native Spanish-speaking US attorney |
| Chinese (中文 简体) | AI-assisted draft | Native Chinese-speaking US attorney |
| Tagalog | AI-assisted draft | Native Tagalog-speaking US attorney |
| Vietnamese (Tiếng Việt) | AI-assisted draft | Native Vietnamese-speaking US attorney |
| Arabic (العربية) | AI-assisted draft | Native Arabic-speaking US attorney |
| French (Français) | AI-assisted draft | Native French-speaking attorney (LA civil law) |

## What needs review

1. Legal terminology accuracy (Bluebook, FRCP, ABA Model Rules, CCPA)
2. Natural phrasing (these are AI drafts — they may sound stilted)
3. Cultural appropriateness (the "second brain" metaphor may not translate)
4. Unicode rendering (Arabic RTL especially)
5. Missing language? Open an issue to request your language.

## How to contribute

1. Read the English authoritative guide: [GETTING_STARTED.md](GETTING_STARTED.md)
2. Pick your language guide from the table above
3. Edit directly and submit a PR to `wolfgang-rush/ai-law-firm-usa`
4. Add your name to the acknowledgments below
