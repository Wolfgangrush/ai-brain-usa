## Currency Warning
Cybersecurity has jumped to the top tier of pain points recently due to the increase in phishing and ransomware attacks targeting small law firms.

## Key Facts
1. **Administrative Overload:** Spending up to 60% of the day on non-billable tasks like billing, collections, and scheduling.
2. **Client Acquisition:** The constant pressure to market and find new clients while maintaining a current caseload ("The Solopreneur's Dilemma").
3. **Cybersecurity Compliance:** Solos are often seen as "soft targets" for hackers. Staying compliant with ethics rules regarding data protection is a major stressor.
4. **"The Lockup":** Unbilled time and unpaid invoices creating cash flow crises.
5. **Technology Fatigue:** The overwhelming number of software options and the lack of an IT department to fix things when they break.
6. **Wellness/Burnout:** High rates of stress and anxiety due to the lack of a support system and the weight of being solely responsible for client outcomes.
7. **Document Drafting Bottlenecks:** Manual drafting of repetitive documents (contracts, motions) taking away from higher-value strategic work.

## Practical Implication for Solo Advocate
Solo advocates are increasingly turning to "Practice OS" tools (unified platforms that combine CRM, billing, and document automation) to solve these pain points. AI is being specifically targeted at the "Document Drafting" and "Administrative" pain points to free up billable hours.
