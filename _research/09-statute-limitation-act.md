## Currency Warning
Statutes of limitations are subject to "tolling" rules which vary by state and claim. Federal "catch-all" period (28 U.S.C. § 1658) applies only to statutes enacted after Dec 1, 1990.

## Key Facts
1. **Federal Criminal (General):** 5 years for most non-capital federal offenses (18 U.S.C. § 3282). Offenses punishable by death or certain terrorism/sex offenses often have no limit or much longer periods.
2. **Federal Civil (Catch-all):** 4 years for civil actions arising under an Act of Congress enacted after Dec 1, 1990 (28 U.S.C. § 1658).
3. **State Civil (Varies widely):**
   - **Personal Injury:** Typically 1–3 years (e.g., 2 years in CA/TX, 3 years in NY).
   - **Breach of Contract (Written):** Typically 4–6 years (e.g., 4 years in CA, 6 years in NY).
   - **Breach of Contract (Oral):** Typically shorter (e.g., 2 years in CA).
   - **Medical Malpractice:** Often 2–3 years with a "discovery rule" (clock starts when the injury was or should have been discovered).
4. **Tolling:** The limitation period may be paused ("tolled") for reasons such as the plaintiff being a minor, being mentally incompetent, or the defendant being out of the state.
5. **Discovery Rule:** In many jurisdictions, the statute of limitations does not begin to run until the plaintiff knows or has reason to know of the injury.
6. **Laches:** An equitable defense in civil cases where unreasonable delay by the plaintiff has prejudiced the defendant, even if the statute of limitations has not yet expired.

## Verbatim Quote (if applicable)
"Except as otherwise expressly provided by law, no person shall be prosecuted, tried, or punished for any offense, not capital, unless the indictment is found or the information is instituted within five years next after such offense shall have been committed." — 18 U.S.C. § 3282(a).

## Practical Implication for Solo Advocate
Missing a statute of limitations is a primary cause of legal malpractice claims. Solo advocates must maintain a robust "statute of limitations" calendar (tickler system) and verify the specific period for every new matter immediately upon intake. When in doubt, filing early is the only safe course.
